RIDING BYTES / Bika Labs - Partnership

Since December 15, 2015 RIDING BYTES and Bika Labs (South Africa) are official Partners.

About

Bika Labs provides up-market Open Source Laboratory Information Management Systems based on the enterprise CMS Plone.

RIDING BYTES supports Bika Labs with interfacing LIMS to other systems and mobile application development.

Looking forward to many interesting projects and a lot of technical opportunities.
